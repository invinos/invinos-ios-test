3. Trust the device on your iPhone and PC

4. Open Sideloadly and drag the `.ipa` file into the app

6. Click **Start** and wait for the installation to finish

7. On your iPhone:

   - Tap your Apple ID
   - Tap **Trust**

8. Done! You can now open and test the Invinos app
